#### Basic Conversation

- なぜなら (nazenara) - Because
- たぶん (tabun) - Maybe
- だから (dakara) - So
- 分かる (wakaru) - I agree or understand (more casual)
- 同意する (dōi suru) - Agree
- 一人で (hitori de) - By myself
- いろいろ (iroiro) - Various
